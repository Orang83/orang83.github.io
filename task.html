Purpose of This Website

Why I Created This Website

The purpose of this website is to explore the still very new world of AI generated images and challenge visitors to try and tell the difference between AI and real images. As AI technologies improve, it becomes more important to understand the impact AI will have. This website serves as an educational platform.
